DESTINY RESTORATION SPORTS ACADEMY: TRAIN LIKE A CHAMPION!

Destiny Restoration Sports Academy, the iconic football club that boasts of great legacy, legendary players and a flamboyant football style, now has football schools in India.

Under Coach Unai Emery, a world renowned manager, Paris Saint-Germain has established itself as a Top 8 European club and is playing an attractive football synonymous with technical excellence, ball possession, blasting attacks and lots of goals! It is the same game philosophy that the Parisian club is applying at youth level in its Academies to train the champions of the future

As part of the club’s commitment to youth development and education, Destiny Restoration SPorts Academy provides youths of Limbe footballers with the opportunity to join a world-class training program right here in India and to step inside the world of the club.
